P5253 [JSOI2013]丢番图

化解：

1x+1y=1n⇒xn+yn=xy⇒xy−xn−yn=0⇒xy−xn−yn+n2=n2⇒(x−n)(y−n)=n2\frac{1}{x}+ \frac{1}{y}= \frac{1}{n} \\ \Rightarrow xn+yn=xy \\ \Rightarrow xy-xn-yn=0 \\ \Rightarrow xy-xn-yn+n^2=n^2 \\ \Rightarrow (x-n)(y-n)=n^2 x1​+y1​=n1​⇒xn+yn=xy⇒xy−xn−yn=0⇒xy−xn−yn+n2=n2⇒(x−n)(y−n)=n2

问题转化为求约数个数，所以枚举所有nnn的质因子的指数qqq，n2n^2n2有2q2q2q个这个因子，所以约数个数×(2q+1)\times(2q+1)×(2q+1)。

P2303 [SDOI2012] Longge 的问题

∑i=1ngcd(i,n)=∑k=1nk∑i=1n[gcd(i,n)==k]=∑k=1nk∑i=1⌊nk⌋[gcd(i,⌊nk⌋)==1]=∑k=1nkϕ(⌊nk⌋)\sum_{i=1}^n gcd(i,n) \\ =\sum_{k=1}^n k\sum_{i=1}^n[gcd(i,n)==k] \\ =\sum_{k=1}^n k\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor\frac{n}{k}\rfloor}[gcd(i,\lfloor\frac{n}{k}\rfloor)==1] \\ =\sum_{k=1}^n k\phi(\lfloor\frac{n}{k}\rfloor) i=1∑n​gcd(i,n)=k=1∑n​ki=1∑n​[gcd(i,n)==k]=k=1∑n​ki=1∑⌊kn​⌋​[gcd(i,⌊kn​⌋)==1]=k=1∑n​kϕ(⌊kn​⌋)

整数分块优化即可。

P2568 GCD

∑i=1n∑j=1n[gcd(i,j)∈prime]=∑p∈primen∑i=1n∑j=1n[gcd(i,j)==p]=∑p∈primen∑i=1⌊np⌋∑j=1⌊np⌋[gcd(i,j)==1]=∑p∈primen∑i=1⌊np⌋2(∑j=1i[gcd(i,j)==1])−1=∑p∈primen∑i=1⌊np⌋2ϕ(i)−1\sum_{i=1}^n \sum_{j=1}^n [gcd(i,j) \in prime] \\ =\sum_{p \in prime}^n \sum_{i=1}^n \sum_{j=1}^n[gcd(i,j)==p] \\ =\sum_{p \in prime}^n \sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{p} \rfloor} \sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{p} \rfloor} [gcd(i,j)==1] \\ =\sum_{p \in prime}^n \sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{p} \rfloor} 2 \left( \sum_{j=1}^i[gcd(i,j)==1] \right)-1 \\ =\sum_{p \in prime}^n \sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{p} \rfloor} 2 \phi(i)-1 i=1∑n​j=1∑n​[gcd(i,j)∈prime]=p∈prime∑n​i=1∑n​j=1∑n​[gcd(i,j)==p]=p∈prime∑n​i=1∑⌊pn​⌋​j=1∑⌊pn​⌋​[gcd(i,j)==1]=p∈prime∑n​i=1∑⌊pn​⌋​2(j=1∑i​[gcd(i,j)==1])−1=p∈prime∑n​i=1∑⌊pn​⌋​2ϕ(i)−1
